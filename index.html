About us

As proud members of the Jewish community, we use our 25+ years of immigration law expertise to best serve our community’s unique needs.

Aron Mandl has helped immigrants achieve their dream of becoming a U.S. citizen. His outside-the-box thinking and creative problem- solving have enabled clients from all walks for life to get their green cards despite seemingly insurmountable challenges. We are honored to be entrusted with changing our clients’ lives in such a wonderful and meaningful way. And while we appreciate cases that go smoothly from beginning to end, we are most proud of those cases we have to fight for.

Practice Areas

We specialize in finding solutions for complex immigration situations, including marriage, family, and divorce due to abuse, asylum, history of criminal background, detained persons, religious worker visas, and entrepreneur/investor visas.
